    Time for a change!


    The right way to do election!!


    For decade’s most of the political parties & politicians have been gathering votes based on caste, religion nepotism, fear. Although with the technological advancements, increase in the national literacy rate & high number of active numbers of first-time voters there has been a paradigm shift in country’s political platform. Voters are looking for a candidate that can deliver what they want as well as deliver it in a way that is beneficial for the society. The old methods of getting votes on the name of caste & religion aren’t working anymore. There have been a some unexpectable result’s in the past few years that has led to change in the conventional political roadmap of the country This project focuses on to get an insight regarding what does the population in a certain constituency needs and will help to raise their concerns and regards to all the candidates running for the office. This will basically focus on people that want to raise a voice and interact to the running candidates but are not able to do so due to their professional commitments.

    On the other hand, it will also guide a roadmap to the candidates regarding the problem faced by the constituency and so that they can design their election manifesto keeping in mind the needs of the constituency. Moreover, it will also give an insight regarding what political party they vote for and what are the factors that affect their choice while voting.

    Please share your thoughs with us so that we can built a community that can make a dent in the system.
